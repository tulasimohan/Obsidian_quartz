# Prefix Parity


>[!Note] Prefix Parity $\PAR_{n,\ell}$ 
> **Alice's input:** $x \in U$.
> **Bob's input:** $y \subseteq U$ of size at most $\ell$.
> **Function:** determine $|\{z \in y| z \leq x\}| \mod 2$.
