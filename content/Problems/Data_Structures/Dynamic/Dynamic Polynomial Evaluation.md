
## Problem Statement

>[!DSP] $P(n,q)$
>**Data**: Polynomials $p$ of degree $n-1$ over the field $\F_q$ represented by their coefficients. 
> **Queries**: for each $x \in \F_q$, outputs $p(x)$ 
> **Updates**: for each $(i,c)$ where $i \in \{0,\dots ,n-1\}$, $c \in \F_q$, replaces the $i^{th}$ coefficient in $p$ by $c$.
