>[!def] **Rank Selection:** 
While not explicitly named, the concept of finding the *k*th smallest element (rank selection) is closely related to the predecessor problem and is relevant to understanding the lower bound arguments.  Finding the predecessor can be seen as a special case of rank selection where you're looking for the element with a rank immediately preceding the query.

>[!DSP] Rank
>Given a set $A \subseteq U$ and element $x \in U$, find the rank of $x$ in $A$.
