
>[!DSP] The orthogonal gap-majority problem 
$\mathrm{ OGMaj}_{n×n}$ is a  promise variant of ${\rm OVC}_{n×n}$ , where we have 
>- $f (x; y) = 1$ if at least  $n/ 2 + √n$ of the rows of $x$ are orthogonal to $y$, and 
>- $f (x; y) = 0$ if no  more than $n/2 − √n$ of the rows of $x$ are orthogonal to $y$, 
>  
with the  promise that we are in one of the two cases.
