# Fully dynamic connectivity in O(log n(log log n)2) amortized expected time


>[!meta]- Abstract
Dynamic connectivity is one of the most fundamental problems in dynamic graph algorithms. We present a randomized Las Vegas dynamic connectivity data structure with O(n(n)²) amortized expected update time and O(n/n) worst case query time, which comes very close to the cell probe lower bounds of Patrascu and Demaine (2006) and Patrascu and Thorup (2011).
